Growing Up

I was born in Malolos, Bulacan a city towards the northern part of the Philippines. I moved to the United States when I was 3 years old and spent most of my life in Chicago, Illinois. I went to elementary and middle school at Charles N. Holden Elementary, a school that holds a special place in my heart. At Charles N. Holden, my passion for technology began.
